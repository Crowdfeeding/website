Crowdfeeding is a project developed by the DeGroote School of Business at McMaster University to tackle hunger and food insecurity. This innovative solution streamlines food rescue operations and ensures fresh food reaches Canadians in need.

We built a cross-platform mobile application that connects food banks, retailers, and individuals in need with our network of volunteer drivers. By leveraging advanced last-mile route optimization and scheduling models, we facilitate efficient, batched delivery routes.
